The colorful coral reefs bustle with activity as schools of fish glide between the swaying anemones. Sharks circle silently, ever alert for prey. From the tiniest seahorse to the mightiest whale, the creatures of the sea choreograph an eternal underwater ballet. The ocean comes alive with movement and sound, a symphony conducted by marine life.
